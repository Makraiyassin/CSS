European Cats

European Shorthair, called the European cat is a cat breed originating in Sweden.

The term has also been used as an elaborate way of referring to common domestic cats of Europe, causing some confusion as the pedigree cats of this breed also should resemble the typical domestic cats of Europe.

Persian Cats

Persian cat is a long-haired breed of cat characterized by its round face and short muzzle. It is also known as the "Persian Longhair" in the English-speaking countries.

siamese Cats

Siamese cat is one of the first distinctly recognized breeds of Asian cat. Derived from the Wichianmat landrace, one of several varieties of cat native to Thailand , the original Siamese became one of the most popular breeds in Europe and North America in the 19th century.
